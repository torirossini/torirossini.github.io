Cyberdrive 2077

iOS and Android

An intense fast-paced, action-packed and arcade-style endless runner. Race a car through a neon digital landscape at breakneck speeds and drive through endless and challenging obstacles that block your path. Built using Unity and C#. Inspired by Cuberunner and Race the Sun. The android version is not currently available on the Google Play store, but the iOS version is available on the App Store for free.

A cooking simulation-style minigame suite with a social media spin. The sixth installment in the Cooking Mama game series, this game seeks to recreate the motion controls of the Nintendo Wii through the Switch's JoyCons and PlayStation 4 motion controls.
