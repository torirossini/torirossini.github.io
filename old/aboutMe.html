Hello, hello!

I'm Tori Rossini, and I do a lot of things.

I'm a gameplay programmer, a game designer, and an automation engineer. My career goals currently are to join a larger game company starting in engineering, eventually transitioning into a project management role. While I enjoy programming, I enjoy organizing teams and facilitating them to have the most success they possibly can. However, in order to do that, I want to have more experience as an engineer or a designer!

I am heavily involved in Computer Science House at RIT, a living-learning organization. In this organization, I served as the Social Director for the 2018-19 term, and was able to accomplish a lot to further and preserve the organization. Now, I've picked up the role of project manager and art team lead for our project for the Imagine RIT festival.

Whenever I'm not working, I'm usually playing video games or having friends over for dinner parties. I just love to cook for people! I also love streaming on Twitch with friends, and playing visual novels with groups of people and voice acting them. I also enjoy drawing, so if I have the opportunity, I love to do different kinds of art for games or for fridge doodles. Maybe I'll toss some up on this site later...

I'm currently in my third year of college, studying Game Design and Development major at Rochester Institute of Technology. I will graduate in May 2021.
